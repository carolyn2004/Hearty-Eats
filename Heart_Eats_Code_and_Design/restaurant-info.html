Paradise Dynasty

cuisine

Dining at Paradise Dynasty is akin to taking a step back to imperial China. Marvel at the opulence and majesty of the restaurant settings while immersing yourself in the finest of northern and southern China cuisine. Touted as a world-first, Paradise Dynasty is proud to present eight first-of-its-kind legendary xiao long bao with flavours such as original, luffa gourd, foie gras, black truffle, crab roe, cheese, garlic and Szechuan. Accompanying these flavourful delicacies are Spicy Szechuan Crispy Chicken, Poached Sliced Fish in Szechuan Chilli Oil, silky La Mian, hot and cold appetisers and a variety of seafood, vegetable and rice dishes.
