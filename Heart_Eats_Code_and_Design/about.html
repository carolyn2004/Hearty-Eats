About

Hearty Eats is a restaurant reservation website created to provide customers with a fuss free dining experience. Book a restaurant anytime, anywhere. Choose a restaurant, select a date and time, and you're good to go!

Frequently asked questions

Is it free to book a table using Hearty Eats?

Using Hearty Eats is completely free! That means diners can book a table at no cost.

How do I cancel my booking?

Go to your profile. There will be an option to view all the bookings you have made so far. From there, you can choose to cancel your reservation.
